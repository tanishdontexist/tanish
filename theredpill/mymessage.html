    • My Message To The World.

    My message to the world is to be loyal and loving to everyone, and to build trust with others while also earning their trust. Money and material things are human-made and shouldn’t be the ultimate goals in life. As humans, our real goal is to seek divine knowledge and understand our own beliefs about God. Often, people who spend a lot of time searching for God end up questioning their beliefs and becoming atheists because they haven’t found what they were looking for. Blindly following others without personal research isn't helpful; instead, we should question everything and do our own research. This world is full of lies, and both you and I can uncover the truth. The education system might not be as beneficial as it seems, and religions and concepts of God often control us through their teachings. If there were a real afterlife or a divine being, good people wouldn’t suffer or die. These issues suggest that perhaps there isn’t an afterlife or a god. Some might argue that good people suffer or die because they did wrong things in a past life, but think about this: if you were put in jail and told it was for something you did in a past life, would you accept it? You wouldn’t. So why accept this explanation when it comes to God? Start thinking critically and logically rather than following things blindly. Stop following harmful influences like K-pop idols or influencers who spread blind beliefs or promote nudity.
